How To Serialize Multiple AND Different Types of Objects in Java

Data to Save:
1. My name (string)
2. My age (int)
3. My hobbies (array of hobbies)
		Name of hobby (string)
		Location of hobby (string)
4. My friends (array of friends)
		Name of friend (string)
		Age of friend (int)
		Hobbies of friend (array of hobbies)
			Name of hobby (string)
			Location of hobby (string)
			
Classes Needed
1. Friend
2. Hobby
3. Main (creates the data, methods)
4. SaveData (calling main to write to file)
5. LoadData (calling main to read from file).
